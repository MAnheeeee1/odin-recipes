How to cook the perfect lasagna

I dont have a single clue honestly, but dont worry we will use the best tool knows to man Google

Ingredients you will need;

  • 3 egg
  • 1 dl milk
  • 100g cheese
  • 1 tsp rosemarine
  • 4 pre made lasagna sheet

How to cook

  1. Pre heat the oven to 200 degree
  2. Cook the meet
  3. Dice the meet
  4. Bland all the spices mentioned above into a bowl with milk
  5. Take the lasagna sheet and spread the souce on it
  6. sprinkle the cheese on top and put it in the oven for 20min

Then you are done Bone apiti!
